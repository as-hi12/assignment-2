Noodle and moonlight

A cozy corner where the rich flavors of authentic Japanese ramen come to life,step into a world of savory broths, tender noodles, and soul-warming comfort.

Ingredients

  • Ramen noodles
  • chicken broth
  • Soft-boiled eggs
  • Sliced chashu pork
  • Green onions

Recipe

  1. Heat the broth until simmering.
  2. Cook ramen noodles and drain.
  3. Cook ramen noodles and drain.
  4. Place noodles in a bowl and pour broth over.
  5. op with sliced pork, eggs, and green onions.
  6. Serve hot and enjoy!
